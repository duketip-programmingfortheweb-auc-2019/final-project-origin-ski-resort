Snow. Fresh, like it should be.

Some advanced skiers don't like their path paved before they go there. That is why Heli Skiing is one of the most popular reasons why experienced skiers come to Origin Ski Resort. We have the best heli skiing services in the entire nation, and we've proved that we are the best. For the past seven years, we have gotten first place in best Heli Skiing services and we don't plan to stop there. We just keep getting better and better. A helicopter picks you up from the base of the resort and takes you to any one of our seven peaks where you can pave your own path, and make your own mark on our resort. And with the helicopter pilot staying in the air watching you the entire time, you know that you are in the safest of hands. Warning. Only attempt Heli Skiing if you are highly experienced. This is extremely dangerous.

Mt. Coquille

This unexplored mountain contains fresh snow whiter than an egg. The terrain is as uneven as a shell, and the amount of jumps will shock you. Only the most experienced skiers will stand a chance on this mountain.

Mt. Noua

On this mountain, there are lots of steep slopes, one even being 45 degrees! However, experts will have a blast weaving through a forest of 360 trees, and jumping over 9 feet in the air.

Mt. Filator

This mountain is incredibly icey, so if you attempt it, be careful not to spin out of control. If it does occur, our staff in the helicopter will save you in a jiffey. But if you are good enough, you'll have a blast here.

Mt. Tombol

This mountain frequently gets hit by blizzards, so there is often an excess amount of snow. Even the most experienced of skiers can sometimes find themselves stuck in the snow.

Mt. Whitewater

On this mountain, there are tons of trees and natural mogles, a perfect site for pro skiers to have fun. This mountain is complete with also being the tallest peak in all of Colorado.

Mt. Tabla

On Mt. Tabla, there is fresh snow each and every day. It's some of the best snow in the country, so our customers take the fullest oppurtunity and sometimes go down this peak twice.

2 Hour Session

This package includes 2 hours of skiing fun! For the low price of $299.99, the helicopter for the 2 hour session leaves at 9:30 am. The helicopter will take about 15 minutes to get to the mountain. This session ends at 11:30 am, the perfect time to get lunch. Our pilots watching from the air will be taking pictures, which you can buy back at the resort for the low, low price of $19.99! Origin Ski Resort is not responsible for any injuries whatsoever. We can assure you that you will always be safe, as our policy states. Have fun skiing!

4 Hour Session

This session is our most popular package! For only $399.99, this is the perfect combination of our 2 hour sessions and our 6 hour sessions. With this session, not only do you get to go to four peaks, but you also get lunch on one of the highest peaks in Colorado. As the skiers and snowboarders go down the mountain, our highly trained pilots will be watchinig them from the air, making sure that the customers safety is ensured, and that they are completely safe. Starting at 9:30 am, the helicopter takes about 20 minutes to get to the peak, and then gives the customer plenty of time to ski to their will. After the customer reaches the bottom of the peak, the pilot will take them to another peak.

6 hour session

You get the best value out of this package! For only $449.99, you get 6 hours of completely fresh snow. Starting at 8:30 am, the pilot will reach the first peak in about 20 minutes, and will cautiously watch the skier or snowboarder go down the mountain. Our pilots are highly trained operatives that ensure the customers safety at all times. They will get to go to 6 peaks, and also have lunch on top of one of the tallest peaks in Colorado.
